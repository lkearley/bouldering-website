6 Steps to Start Climbing

1. Buy climbing shoes and chalk or rent some from your climbing gym. Shoes should be very snug but not painful.

Climbing Shoes

Chalk Bag

2. Find a climbing gym, Stone Summit Midtown is a good gym and is walking distance from Georgia Tech campus.

Interior of a Climbing Gym

3. Once you are at the gym, find the starting point label for a route you can climb. This label will tell you how difficult the route is from V0 - V17.

Difficulty label

4. Look at the problem and try to envision your intended route to the end. You can only use rock with the same color as the one with the starting label.

Green Climbing Route

5. Start climbing! Do not climb higher than you are comfortable falling from.

Correct Climbing Technique

6. If you begin to fall, lean back and try to land softly on your feet with bent knees and roll backward.

Correct Falling Technique

Still confused? The gear, gym, and technique pages have more information on the fundimentals of bouldering. Also Stone Summit Midtown has free introductory classes which will give you a hands on experience.
