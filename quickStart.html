5 Steps to Start Climbing

  • 1. Buy climbing shoes and chalk or rent some from your climbing gym. Shoes should be very snug but not painful.
  • 2. Find the starting point label for a problem, these labels will tell you how difficult the problem is from V0 - V17
  • 3. Look at the problem and try to envision your intended route to the end
  • 4. Start climbing using only rocks of the same color as the starting rock
  • 5. If you begin to fall, lean back and try to land softly on your feet with bent knees and roll backward.
